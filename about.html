About Us & Our Skills

Welcome to Meyflower's – Where flower speaks louder than words;

Our journey began with a passion for the timeless elegance of flowers, inspired by the delicate beauty of the original Mayflower bloom. The name Meyflower is a harmonious fusion of 'Mey,' a tribute to the month of May when nature awakens with a kaleidoscope of colors, and an ode to the ship that embarked on a historic floral journey.

Founded by Yem, passion for flowers, coupled with the nickname that adds a personal touch to our brand, it infuses each creation with warmth, care, and a touch of uniqueness. Meyflower's is more than just a flower shop; it's a celebration of nature's artistry.
